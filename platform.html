Microservices Cloud Architecture

Composable services, independent scaling, resilient by design.

Why microservices?

  • Independent scaling (e.g., Emissions Engine under load)
  • Fault isolation for higher resilience
  • Faster, team-level deploys (service ownership)
  • API-first integrations across the stack

Trade-offs

  • Operational complexity (observability, tracing)
  • Network overhead vs. monolith
  • Requires strong DevOps (CI/CD, IaC)
