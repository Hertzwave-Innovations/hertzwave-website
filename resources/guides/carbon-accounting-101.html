Scopes Explained

Most frameworks follow the GHG Protocol...

Scope 1 — Direct Emissions

  • Stationary combustion: boilers, furnaces, gensets
  • Mobile combustion: company-owned vehicles, construction equipment
  • Fugitive emissions: refrigerant leaks (R134a, R410a), SF₆

Scope 2 — Indirect Electricity

Reported two ways: location-based and market-based...

Scope 3 — Value Chain

  • Purchased goods & services
  • Capital goods
  • Business travel, commuting, transport & distribution
  • Use-phase & end-of-life

Data Hierarchies

  1. Activity data × specific EF
  2. Supplier-specific emission factors
  3. Industry averages (Ecoinvent, DEFRA)
  4. Spend-based estimates (least preferred)

Sample Calculation

Diesel consumed: 12,000 litres
EF (DEFRA 2024): 2.54 kg CO₂e/litre
Total = 12,000 × 2.54 = 30.48 tCO₂e

Controls & Assurance

  • Maintain versioned emission factors with sources
  • Track data lineage
  • Variance analysis vs prior periods
