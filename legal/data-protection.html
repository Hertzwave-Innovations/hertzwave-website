Data Protection

Our approach to safeguarding customer data.

Lawful Basis: Contract performance, legitimate interests, and consent where applicable.

Subprocessors: Disclosed on request; all subprocessors are bound by security and confidentiality terms.

International Transfers: Appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) where required.

Incident Response: A defined plan with timely notifications aligned to legal obligations.
