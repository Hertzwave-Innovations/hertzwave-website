Data Protection

Our approach to safeguarding customer data.

Lawful Basis: Contract performance, legitimate interests and consent where applicable.

Subprocessors: Disclosed on request; all bound by security and confidentiality terms.

International Transfers: Appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) where required.

Incident Response: Defined plan with timely notifications aligned to legal obligations.
