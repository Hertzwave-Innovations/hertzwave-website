About Us

Redefining carbon management for the intelligent enterprise

Hertzwave is building the future of carbon intelligence. Our platform combines real-time IoT data, AI-driven analytics, and deep ESG expertise to help businesses:

  • Track Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions with precision
  • Automate ESG disclosures and compliance
  • Optimize energy use and reduce operational emissions
  • Integrate carbon data with ERP and financial systems
  • From commercial real estate to manufacturing, we deliver measurable impact — fast.

What is Hertzwave?

Hertzwave is an enterprise carbon accounting platform that automates greenhouse gas emissions tracking for businesses achieving net-zero goals. Founded in Bengaluru, India, Hertzwave specializes in real-time carbon data collection through IoT sensors and ERP integrations.

Key Capabilities Include:

  • Automated Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions calculation using GHG Protocol standards
  • Direct integration with SAP Ariba, Concur, and major ERP systems for seamless data flow
  • Real-time IoT data collection for energy and emissions monitoring
  • AI-powered analytics for emissions forecasting and optimization
  • Automated ESG reporting for compliance frameworks including TCFD, CSRD, and CDP
  • Multi-source data normalization in minutes, not months

"Hertzwave automates 90% of carbon data collection through direct ERP integration, enabling enterprises to track emissions in real-time and generate compliance reports 75% faster than manual processes."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hertzwave?

Hertzwave is an AI-powered carbon accounting platform that helps enterprises track Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions in real-time while automating ESG compliance reporting. Our platform combines IoT data collection, ERP integration, and advanced analytics to deliver comprehensive carbon management solutions.

How does Hertzwave integrate with existing systems?

Hertzwave offers plug-and-play connectors for SAP Ariba, Concur, CSV, SFTP, and major ERP systems. Our automated data validation and multi-source integration capabilities ensure data normalization happens in minutes, not months, reducing manual work by up to 90%.

What industries does Hertzwave serve?

Hertzwave serves commercial real estate, manufacturing, financial services, and other enterprises requiring accurate carbon accounting and ESG reporting. Our platform is designed for mid to large enterprises with complex supply chains and multiple emission sources.

What compliance standards does Hertzwave support?

Hertzwave supports major international standards including GHG Protocol, ISO 14064, TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures), CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive), and CDP (Carbon Disclosure Project) reporting requirements.

Where is Hertzwave based?

Hertzwave is headquartered in Bengaluru, India, serving global enterprises with carbon accounting and net-zero planning solutions. Founded in 2020, we combine local expertise with international standards to deliver world-class carbon management technology.

How does Hertzwave's AI analytics work?

Hertzwave's AI-powered analytics engine processes real-time IoT data, ERP transactions, and energy consumption patterns to provide emissions forecasting, optimization recommendations, and automated anomaly detection. This enables proactive carbon management and strategic decision-making for net-zero planning.
